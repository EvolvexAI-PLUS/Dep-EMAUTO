Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with EmailAI?
Simply visit our homepage and click "Get Started" to sign up for a free trial. You'll need to connect your email account through our secure OAuth integration.
What email providers do you support?
We currently support Microsoft Outlook, Google Gmail, and IMAP-compatible email servers. We're working on adding support for more providers.
Is my email data secure?
Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations. Your email content is only used to generate responses and is never shared or sold.
Can I customize the AI responses?
Yes! You can set your tone preferences, signature, primary business details, and other personalization settings in your profile. The AI learns your unique communication style over time.
What if I need to cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime through your account settings. We offer a full refund within the first 30 days. Just contact our support team for assistance.

Technical Support

For technical issues, please provide as much detail as possible in your support request. This includes:
  • Error messages or screenshots
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Your browser type and version
  • Your email provider (Outlook, Gmail, etc.)
  • The time and date when the issue occurred
